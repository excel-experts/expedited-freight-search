üìã CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should include the following columns:

  • order_id - Unique identifier from SuperDispatch
  • pickup_business - The business name where the vehicle(s) will be picked up.
  • delivery_business - The business name where the vehicle(s) will be dropped off.
  • pickup_city - Pickup city and state (e.g., "Los Angeles, CA")
  • delivery_city - Delivery city and state (e.g., "New York, NY")
  • carrier - Carrier name
  • carrier_price - Price paid to the Carrier (numeric)
  • tarriff_price - Customer Payment Total Tarriff - Payment made to us by the customer. (numeric)
  • distance - Distance in miles (numeric)
  • order_date - Order date (YYYY-MM-DD format)

Note: price_per_mile will be automatically calculated if not provided.
